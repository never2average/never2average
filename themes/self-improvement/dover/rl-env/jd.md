# RL QA Intern, Dover RL Environment

[Dover](../overview.md) learns to think like an attacker inside a reinforcement learning environment. That environment is the whole game: if it is even slightly wrong, the agent learns the wrong instincts. Keeping it honest is real, foundational work.

I want someone with genuine RL or environment-design experience who also has the temperament to break things methodically until only the truth survives.

## What you will do
- Stress-test the RL environment design for correctness and edge cases.
- Find where reward, state, or sandboxing diverge from the real world.
- Build the repeatable suite that keeps the environment trustworthy as it evolves.

## You are
- Experienced with RL setups or environment design.
- A rigorous tester at heart who enjoys dismantling a system to prove it.

## The deal

Paid stipend. Part-time and flexible around your life. Remote, global. If you are exceptional, this converts to a full-time or founding role.

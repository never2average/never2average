# Research Intern (CS PhD depth), Immaculate v2

The deepest question in [Immaculate](../overview.md) is whether taste can truly compound without quietly drifting away from what "right" means. v2 is where that question lives. This is not a volume role; it is a thinking role, a few focused hours a week with someone who can reason about a self-improving system from first principles.

If you are doing or have done a PhD in ML or RL, and the idea of a training loop that judges its own taste makes you want to find the flaw in it, come argue with me.

## What you will do
- Pressure-test the taste fine-tune and the Recruiter and Trainer loop for soundness.
- Help me reason about drift, measurement, and whether the compounding is real.
- Go deep, not wide.

## You are
- A CS PhD student or equivalent depth in ML, RL, or a closely related field.
- The kind of person who critiques a system to make it true, not to win.

## The deal

Paid stipend. Part-time and flexible around your life. Remote, global. If you are exceptional, this converts to a full-time or founding role.

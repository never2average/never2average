# Immaculate

Agents with taste, not just output. ([useimmaculate.com](https://useimmaculate.com))

Real research is expensive. A single hypothesis can cost hundreds of millions of tokens, so a researcher on a fixed budget gets only a handful of honest shots, and that scarcity is exactly what forges taste. It is also why brilliant people walk away when the compute is not there. I want to hand that freedom to an agent. Immaculate steers many experiments at once, each with its own budget and constraints written in a per-product DSL, fans hypotheses out as cheap diffs, reviews them on its own loop, and pays the real cost of code generation only on the branch it believes in. Taste is that judgment, the token efficiency of reaching the right answer, and it compounds with every run.

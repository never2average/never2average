# Self-improving CFO

Inside a company almost nothing is verifiable except code, and the one thing that grounds everything else is finance. As companies become agent-run, money becomes the honest signal: token-spend ROI is what decides what gets done, what gets scaled, and what gets let go. I am building toward a CFO that improves itself, where a founder steers a swarm with a single sentence and the agents carry the company's financial reality from there.

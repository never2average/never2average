# Vibe coding a company

A company is really a harness: a way for many people to share responsibility and aim swarm intelligence at a founder's objective. The wave of digital-twin and run-your-own-business tools promised exactly this and never delivered, because they bolt a chat box onto dashboards instead of letting language genuinely run the company. I want to vibe-code the company itself, where you describe the objective and the harness composes the agents and workflows that chase it.

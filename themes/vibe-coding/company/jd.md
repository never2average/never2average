# Research Collaborator, Vibe Coding a Company

A company is really a harness: a way for people to share responsibility and point swarm intelligence at a founder's objective. Every "run your business with AI" tool so far has faked this by bolting a chat box onto dashboards. I want to actually do it, from a collection of agents (v1) to an operating-system-style product (v2) where language genuinely runs the company.

This is open research, and I am looking for an equal, not an errand-runner. The one thing I care about most is that you are an original thinker who will push back when I am wrong.

## What you will do
- Co-design the agents and the system that turn a stated objective into composed workflows.
- Own real problems end to end and shape the direction with me.
- Disagree, loudly, with evidence.

## You are
- A genuinely original thinker with your own worldview on agents and how companies work.
- Ready to take real ownership of an undefined, foundational problem.

## The deal

Paid stipend. Part-time and flexible around your life. Remote, global. If you are exceptional, this converts to a full-time or founding role.

## Apply

Open an [issue](../../../../issues) and pitch me: who you are, and why this is yours. No forms.
